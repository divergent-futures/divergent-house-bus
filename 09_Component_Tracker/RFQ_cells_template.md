# RFQ — Sodium-ion cells (ready to paste to suppliers)

**Use this to request quotes yourself** (I can't send it for you). Send to tier-1 makers / their distributors and a few Alibaba Gold suppliers, then paste the replies into the tracker. Aim for **3–5 quotes** to compare.

## Who to send it to
- **HiNa Battery** (Beijing / Zhongke Haina) — the 200 Ah cell's maker; ask for a direct distributor.
- **BYD** — has its own 200 Ah sodium cell + ESS channel.
- **Veken Technology** — China's #1 sodium shipper (H1 2025).

- **Alibaba** — search "sodium ion battery 200Ah prismatic," filter **Verified/Gold + Trade Assurance**, message 3–4.

## The message (edit the bracketed bits)

> Subject: RFQ — 650× sodium-ion prismatic cells (3.0 V, ~200 Ah) for an EV/ESS build
>
> Hello,
>
> I'm sourcing cells for a custom electric vehicle / stationary storage build (open-source project). I'd like a quote on your **3.0 V ~200 Ah prismatic sodium-ion cell** (e.g. HiNa NaCP73174207-ME200 or your equivalent).
>
> **Quantity:** 650 cells (~390 kWh). Please also quote price breaks at **1,000** and **2,000** cells.
>
> Please provide:
> 1. **Price** per cell and **per kWh**, both **EXW** and **CIF [your port/country]**.
> 2. **MOQ** and current **lead time** for 650 units.
> 3. **Cell grading/matching** — are cells capacity- and IR-sorted and delivered **matched** (needed for a ~133S series pack)? What ΔmV / Δmohm tolerance?
> 4. **Datasheet** + verified specs: capacity, Wh/kg, cycle life at 1C to 80%, temperature range, internal resistance.
> 5. **Certifications:** UN38.3 (shipping), IEC 62619 / GB, and any UL if available.
> 6. **Shipping:** as Class 9 hazmat — do you handle export packaging + docs? Sea vs air options.
> 7. **Payment terms** and whether **Trade Assurance** (Alibaba) applies.
> 8. **Samples** — can I buy 2–4 cells first to test before the full order?
> 9. Do you also offer a **larger cell (e.g. 270 Ah)** — quote that per kWh too.
>
> Thank you — I can share the pack spec on request.

## What to watch for (grading the replies)
- **Matched cells matter** for a big series pack — an unmatched batch costs you capacity + balancing headache. Insist on sorted/matched.
- **$/kWh is the comparison metric**, not $/cell (capacities vary). Target **~$80–105/kWh** cell-level; below that is great, well above suggests small-qty markup.
- **Certs (UN38.3)** are mandatory to ship — no cert, no deal.
- **Buy samples first.** Test capacity + IR on a few before committing ~$35–40k.
- Beware listings far below market ($/piece that implies <$40/kWh) — likely mislabeled or a scam.

## Rough budget (from research, not a quote)
650 cells × 600 Wh = 390 kWh × ~$80–105/kWh = **~$31k–$41k** for the cells (this build's single biggest line). Add BMS, busbars, and the enclosure separately.
