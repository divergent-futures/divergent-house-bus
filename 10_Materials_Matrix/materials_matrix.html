DF Materials Matrix

One 1–5 scorecard for every material, vehicle-dwelling weighted. Sort by any column; click a name for pros/cons. Data: materials_matrix.js.

Weights: performance/lightness ×1.5 · durability/health ×1.2 · cost/workability ×1.0 · sustainability ×0.8. Green = strong, red = weak. The weighted total is a guide — always read the axes for the specific job.
