## 1. Purpose & requirements
A lightweight structural shell with the sodium pack as the structural floor, targeting a 20-25% mass strip off a typical RV via advanced materials and smart engineering.

## 3. Components
- Structural pack spine + aluminium/composite stub rails (front + rear crash structure)
- Carbon/glass-over-foam body panels; EPP foam interior with CF skins
- Vacuum-panel / aerogel insulation; double-layer polycarbonate glazing + concealed insulated drop-down covers
- Adjustable air suspension; slide-outs for interior space

n/a (structural).
