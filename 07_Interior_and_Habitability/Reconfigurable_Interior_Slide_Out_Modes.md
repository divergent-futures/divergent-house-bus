# Reconfigurable Interior — Slide-Outs as Live/Work/Exercise Space

**Date:** 2026-07-03  ·  V2 bus (concept, TJ). The slide-outs aren't just for sleeping room — **the further they extend, the more utility space you gain**, and the interior **reconfigures by mode** through the day. This is the "mobile live-work" positioning (huge for the remote-worker/creator personas). Feeds floor-plan v0.7 + Controls.

---

## 1. The idea
Slide-outs (lounge/galley + bedroom) extend for **daytime utility**, not only night-time sleeping space. You choose which to deploy, when. Modes:
- **Morning / exercise:** extend the **bedroom** slide (one side) → open floor for **yoga / stretching / a workout** (TJ's wife's morning routine).
- **Work / dual office:** extend the **lounge** slide → a **portable office** — **screens (not a TV)**, standing/fold-down desk. Both partners can work at once: **she** at a laptop + **fold-down table** in the bedroom, **he** in the lounge — **or outside** (exterior power + a fold-out setup).
- **Social / relax:** lounge slide out, dinette/sofa mode.
- **Sleep:** you *don't* need the slides out to sleep — the fixed footprint already fits the bed — so sliding is about *activity space*, not just the bed.

## 2. Why it's strong
- **Live-work is the point for full-timers** — a real, two-person mobile office + gym + home, not a cramped camper. Directly serves the **content-creator / remote-worker** persona (and TJ + wife themselves).
- **Screens, not a TV** — modular multi-screen walls double as a work display and media; fits the all-DC, no-single-purpose-appliance ethos.
- **Fold-down / stowable furniture** — desks, tables, and the exercise floor stow away, so one space serves many modes (extends the Signature transforming ethos beyond the bathroom).

## 3. Considerations
- **Slide travel vs utility:** the further out, the more floor — but more thermal-bridge, weight, seal, and travel-position constraints. Balance extent against the winter thermal penalty (occasional-winter decision already accepts this).
- **Structure:** slide floors carry live loads (a person doing yoga, a desk) — size accordingly.
- **Power/data at the work zones:** DC outlets + USB-C PD + data (Starlink/LTE) at the lounge desk, the bedroom desk, and an **exterior work point** (ties to the bidirectional/V2X exterior outlets).
- **Controls:** which slide, when — a "mode" button (sleep / work / exercise / social) that also sets lighting, climate zone, and screens. Feeds the controls brain.
- **Both variants:** applies to Standard and Signature (it's layout + furniture, not the bathroom fork).
